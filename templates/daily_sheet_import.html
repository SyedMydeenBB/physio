Excel Format Instructions

Your Excel file should have the following columns:

  • Date - Format: YYYY-MM-DD
  • Name - Patient name
  • Case Number - Unique case identifier
  • Diagnosis - Medical diagnosis
  • Charge - Amount charged (numeric)
  • Received - Amount received (numeric)
  • Payment Status - Options: Paid, Partially Paid, Not Paid
  • Payment Type - Options: QR, Cash
  • Payment Frequency - Options: Daily Basis, Weekly Basis, Monthly Basis
  • In Time - Format: HH:MM
  • Out Time - Format: HH:MM
  • Treatment 1, Treatment 2, Treatment 3, Treatment 4 - Optional
  • Therapist 1 - Options: Dr. Basidh, Dr. Visitra, Dr. Bharatiselvi
  • Therapist 2 - Optional (same options as Therapist 1)
